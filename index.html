How spells are mastered
Every skill is treated like a spell. The game tracks accuracy by spell, saves mistakes by topic, and quietly mixes those spells back into later levels until the accuracy reaches 100%. Then the spell is marked as mastered.
The game can save spell progress in the browser and also sync it to Supabase, so mastery pages can follow the story across devices that use the same child name.

        📜 Adventure Guide

        A simple guide to stars, spells, reviews, and Bonus Boss rewards.

        How each story stop works

        • Each level has 12 questions.
        • About 5 questions are old spells for review.
        • About 7 questions are the newest spell work for this chapter.
        • Old spells come back in deeper, more varied ways.

        Main level stars

        • 11–12 correct → 3 stars
        • 8–10 correct → 2 stars
        • 5–7 correct → 1 star
        • 0–4 correct → 0 stars

        Spell mastery

        Each knowledge skill is a spell. A spell is mastered only when that spell's accuracy reaches 100%. Wrong answers quietly return later in spiral review.

        Bonus Boss rewards

        Every few levels, a real AMC 8 Bonus Boss appears. Open the real problem, use Sir Knight's hints, and then claim the extra bonus stars once after solving it.

        Bonus stars are added on top of normal level stars.
