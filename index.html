Adventure Guide

  • Each main story level uses 12 questions.
  • About 40% are older spells for review, and about 60% are newer spells introduced gently.
  • Spirit Stones come from main level results and AMC Bonus Boss rewards.
  • A spell is mastered when its current accuracy reaches 100%.
  • When a spell is new, Sir Knight gives a lesson page before the level starts.
  • Bonus Boss levels use AMC 8 real-problem references, plus original knight hints.
