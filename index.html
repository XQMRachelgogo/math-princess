How spells are mastered
Every skill is treated like a spell. The game tracks accuracy by spell, groups mistakes by topic, and keeps bringing that spell back until the accuracy reaches 100%. Then the spell is marked as mastered.
The game can save spell progress in the browser and also sync it to Supabase, so mastery pages can follow the story across devices that use the same child name.
